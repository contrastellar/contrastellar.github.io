Who am I?

Author, Musician, Software Developer
Full-Time IT Specialist, Part-Time Student
I've been a writer and storyteller for most of my life, and grew up in a household that encouraged me to pursue creativity wheresoever I can.
As a consequence, I've been a semi-professional writer since 2022, and have been a practicing, hobbyist musician since 2017. Music, I would say,
is the harder of the two practices.
